ICC world cup 2024

The 2024 ICC Men's T20 World Cup is the ninth edition of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup,
a biennial Twenty20 International (T20I) tournament contested by men's national teams and organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC).
It is being co-hosted by the West Indies and the United States from 1 to 29 June 2024.
[1] It is the first major ICC event tournament to feature matches played in the United States,
and in any other country in the Americas outside the West Indies
